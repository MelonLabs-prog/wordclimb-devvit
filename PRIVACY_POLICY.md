# Privacy Policy for WordClimb

**Last Updated: November 11, 2025**

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to WordClimb! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you play WordClimb ("the Game"), a Reddit Devvit application.

Your privacy is important to us. This policy describes what data we collect and how we use it.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Automatically Collected Information
When you play WordClimb, we automatically collect:
- **Reddit Username**: Your public Reddit username for displaying on leaderboards
- **Reddit User ID**: Your unique Reddit identifier for tracking game progress
- **Game Progress**: Your current puzzle state, word path, and completion status
- **Scores**: Your scores and step counts for completed puzzles
- **Timestamps**: When you play and complete puzzles

### 2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect
WordClimb does NOT collect:
- Personal information (email, phone number, real name, etc.)
- Location data
- Device information beyond what Reddit provides
- Browsing history
- Data from other websites or apps
- Payment information (the Game is free)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:
- **Game Functionality**: Saving your progress, calculating scores, and determining if you've completed puzzles
- **Leaderboards**: Displaying usernames and scores in daily rankings
- **Game Statistics**: Showing you your performance compared to optimal solutions
- **Service Improvement**: Understanding how players use the Game to make improvements

## 4. Data Storage

### 4.1 Where Data is Stored
- All data is stored in **Reddit's Redis database** through the Devvit platform
- Data is stored within Reddit's infrastructure and subject to Reddit's security measures
- We do not store data on external servers or databases

### 4.2 Data Retention
- Daily puzzle data is retained indefinitely for historical leaderboards
- User progress is retained as long as you continue to play
- You can request data deletion by contacting us (see Contact section)

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 5.1 Public Information
The following information is visible to other players:
- Your Reddit username on leaderboards
- Your scores and rankings on completed puzzles
- Number of steps taken to solve puzzles

### 5.2 Third-Party Sharing
We do NOT sell, trade, or share your data with third parties, except:
- **Reddit/Devvit Platform**: Data is stored on Reddit's infrastructure and subject to Reddit's Privacy Policy
- **Legal Requirements**: If required by law or legal process

## 6. Reddit's Role

WordClimb operates on Reddit's Devvit platform. Your use of the Game is also governed by:
- **Reddit's Privacy Policy**: https://www.reddit.com/policies/privacy-policy
- **Reddit's User Agreement**: https://www.redditinc.com/policies/user-agreement

Reddit may collect additional information as described in their policies.

## 7. External Services

### 7.1 Dictionary API
WordClimb may use external dictionary APIs to validate words. These services may log:
- Word queries for validation
- Timestamps of requests
- No personal information is shared with these services

## 8. Children's Privacy

WordClimb is intended for users who meet Reddit's age requirements (13+ in most jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The Game complies with Reddit's age policies.

## 9. Data Security

We implement security measures including:
- Using Reddit's secure infrastructure and APIs
- No collection of sensitive personal information
- Limited data collection to only what's necessary for gameplay
- Following Devvit platform security best practices

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 10. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View your game data by playing the Game
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your game data by contacting us
- **Opt-Out**: Stop using the Game at any time to stop data collection
- **Reddit Privacy Controls**: Use Reddit's privacy settings to manage your Reddit account

### How to Delete Your Data
To delete your WordClimb data:
1. Contact us through the methods listed below
2. Provide your Reddit username
3. We will delete your game progress, scores, and associated data

Note: This will not delete your Reddit account or activity outside of WordClimb.

## 11. International Users

WordClimb is hosted on Reddit's global infrastructure. By using the Game, you consent to the transfer and processing of your data in accordance with Reddit's policies and applicable laws.

## 12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted with a new "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of WordClimb after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
